## 6. Conclusions and recommended actions

- Follow up first with the 68 learners who are active on Typing or Quill but have no DET, EFSet, or resolved Northstar learner record.
- Prioritise assessment clean-up at the 21 schools with platform-active learners missing assessment records; the largest single school gap is 7 learners.
- Use the Typing activity leaders as engagement examples: the top 10 schools generated 5,166 resolved lesson activities across 208 school-level learner counts.
- Give targeted academic support to the 6 high-volume, below-average Quill schools before increasing Quill assignment volume further.
